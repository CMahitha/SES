A Unique Celebration

The uniqueness of Swarastra Ekta Sangamam is celebrating the 75th Republic Day for consecutive 75 Days across 75 Cities.

As India recently celebrated 75 years of independence with the Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav, SES seizes the moment to honor and celebrate it.

 SES- A movement that transcends borders and unites us all.

It symbolizes our journey toward freedom, reminding us of the enduring spirit that defines our nation.

75 Days, 75 Cities

Swarastra Ekta Sangamam is not merely an event; it's a symbol of our collective pride, our shared history, and our hopes for a brighter future.


In an unprecedented endeavor, SES will unfold over 75 days, spanning 75 diverse cities across the Indian subcontinent.

Join us on this unique journey to celebrate India's 75th Republic Day and the rich tapestry of our heritage.
